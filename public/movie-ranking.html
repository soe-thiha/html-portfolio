Top Movies of All Time According to Soe

My best 3 movies.

Cast Away (2000)

Tom Hanks stars as a FedEx executive stranded on a deserted island after a plane crash. The film explores themes of survival, hope, and human resilience.

The Revenant (2015)

DiCaprio's gripping performance as a frontiersman left for dead in the wilderness earned him his first Oscar. The movie is a brutal tale of survival and revenge.

Young Woman and the Sea (2024)

Eventhough some critizie its familiar and predictable storyline, I found the movie inspiring and heartwarming. It tells the true story of a young woman who overcomes societal expectations to pursue her passion for sailing. Critics describe the film as a rousing, emotional sports biopic with an inspiring underdog story.
